Debugging:
---------

Debugging is the process of identifying and resolving errors, or bugs, in a software system. 

It is an important aspect of software engineering because bugs can cause a software system to malfunction,

  and can lead to poor performance or incorrect results.

Step into :
---------
        Debugs line by line.

Step over:
---------
        Debugs the current function at a time.
    
Step out:
--------
        Debugs the whole code at a time and returns result
